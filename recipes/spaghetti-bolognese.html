Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese are one of the most famous Italy pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 3 pcs carrots
  • 3 pcs celery
  • 3 pcs parsley
  • 200ml white wine
  • 2 tbsp Tomate paste
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmiggiano cheese

Steps

  1. Dice the vegetables into small cubes and add them to a saucepan
  2. Once the vegetables are soft and the meat and fry until well browned
  3. Add the white wine and tomate paste and stir until combined
  4. Put some cooked spaghetti on a plate, a big spoon of the finished sauce and sprinkle with grated Parmiggiano cheese
