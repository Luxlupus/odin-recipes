Pizza

Description

Pizza is an amazing combination of pillowy dough, tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pcs pizza dough
  • 100ml tomato pizza sauce
  • 150g mozzarella cheese
  • 3 leaves of basil

Steps

  1. Stretch the pizza dough in a circle
  2. Cover the stretched dough with sauce
  3. Put the cheese and basil on top
  4. Bake the pizza in the oven for 5 minutes
